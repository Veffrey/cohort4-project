About Me

Hello There, I'm Veffrey Okhamera a frontend developer interning and training with hertechtrail.
I build websites and is ready to collaborate with any team ready to work with me

The Experience

The experience so far at hertechtrail was a lucrative one. One i must say i learnt alot from. On the first week we took our fitness test which guaranteed our final entry into the cohort. The weeek after that, we were introduced to the basics of HTML for as time went by, we proceeded to other aspects of frontend development such as CSS, BOOTSTRAP and JAVASCRIPT. We were taught how to use GIT,GITHUB, and NETLIFY to create repositories, fork projects and host sites respectively. In summary it has been a really educative journey.

Challenges Encountered

The challenges I encountered were quite personal. I couldn't meet up with all my classes because I worked on weekends which coincidentally was the time i had classes resulting to me having little or no access to my phone during working hours. I tired by catching up during the week with the recordings from class. In cases where i didn't understand what was being taught, I consulted other materials and google.
